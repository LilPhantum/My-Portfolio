Ingredients: Bread, avocado, lemon, salt, pepper

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture and serve.

Ingredients: Bananas, eggs, flour, milk

  1. Mash bananas and whisk with eggs and flour.
  2. Pour onto hot pan and cook.
  3. Serve with honey or syrup.

Ingredients: Pork, pineapple, tortillas, spices

  1. Marinate pork in spices and pineapple.
  2. Grill and slice.
  3. Assemble in tortilla and serve.

Ingredients: Cocoa, sugar, flour, eggs, butter

  1. Mix ingredients.
  2. Bake for 25 minutes.
  3. Let cool and serve.

Ingredients: Lentils, onions, carrots, spices

  1. Simmer lentils with spices.

  3. Serve hot with bread.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, milk, cocoa powder

  1. Mix ingredients in a mug.
  2. Microwave for 90 seconds.
